Does My Customer Need To Have Booked An Appointment?

No, and that's the whole point. Every other tool requires a booking to exist first. We don't. Just a name, a number, and when they last visited. That's it.

Will It Look Like The Messages Come From Me?

Completely. Messages go out in your name and any replies come straight to your number. ReBookEasy is invisible to your customers.

Can I Add Customers I Haven't Seen In Months?

That's actually where the magic is. Add someone from three years ago and we'll start reminding them. You'd be surprised how many people come back when someone just asks.

What If A Customer Tells Me To Stop?

They reply STOP and it's done immediately. You can also pause or remove any customer from your account with one click. No awkwardness.
